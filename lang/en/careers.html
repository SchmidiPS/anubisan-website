Wound Manager / Nursing Professional (m/f/d)

As a Wound Manager or Nursing Professional, you will play a central role in the care and treatment of our patients. You will work closely with doctors, carry out phase-appropriate wound care, and document the healing process. We offer you the opportunity to deepen your expertise through regular training and to learn about new, innovative treatment methods. Your work will directly contribute to improving the quality of life of our patients and achieving long-term success in wound treatment.
