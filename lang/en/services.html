Our Services

We offer a comprehensive and innovative method for the treatment of chronic wounds. Our goal is to provide patients with optimal healing and quality of life through phase-appropriate wound care.

Wound Analysis

The foundation of every treatment is wound analysis. This involves clarifying the cause and condition of the wound. Treatment can only begin with a doctor’s prescription. Depending on the insurance and the country, the costs of treatment are partially covered.

Phase-Appropriate Treatment

Our treatment is always phase-appropriate, tailored to the specific requirements of the wound. Each wound is photo-documented during the treatment, and adjustments are made in consultation with the treating doctor if necessary. We rely on the latest technologies and proven methods.

The Use of Nekrostat™

Our wound healing accelerator Nekrostat™ is used during treatment to promote sustainable healing. Additionally, we use:
