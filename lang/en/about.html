Our History

Anubisan stands for over three decades of expertise in wound care. Our journey began with the vision to develop innovative solutions for healing chronic wounds. With our unique development of a wound healing accelerator and our expertise in wound diagnosis, we have helped countless patients regain their quality of life.

Our Values

Experience

Decades of expertise in wound care. Through our research and practice, we have built extensive knowledge that benefits patients.

Competence

Prevention of numerous amputations through phase-specific treatment and thorough documentation. Our work is based on scientific methods and innovative approaches.

Collaboration

Close collaboration with doctors and medical professionals, as well as continuous training for our team, to always achieve the best results.

Name and Meaning

The name Anubisan is derived from Anubis, the ancient Egyptian god of healing, and San, which originates from the Latin word "sanare" (to heal). Anubis symbolizes protection, restoration, and renewal, while San highlights our scientific and healing approach. This combination of words merges tradition and modernity, reflecting our philosophy of healing with respect for the past and innovative technologies of the present. Anubisan represents our mission not just to close wounds but to restore quality of life and bring hope to patients.
