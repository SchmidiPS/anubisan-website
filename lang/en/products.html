Our Products

Our products have been developed over years in collaboration with doctors, based on our own unique formula, to support the healing of chronic and complex wounds. With our solutions, we ensure that wounds heal from the inside out, while effectively cleaning the wound bed.

Wound Dressings and Bandage Kit

Our wound dressing and bandage kit includes a special hydrogel that retains moisture in the wound and supports the healing process. The combination of bandage materials ensures optimal care.

Wound Healing Accelerator Nekrostat™

Our specially developed wound healing accelerator Nekrostat™ cleans the wound down to its depths and promotes healing through innovative technology. Nekrostat™ is unique in its mechanism and an essential component of phase-appropriate wound care.

Complete Kit

Our complete kit combines all the components for optimal wound care: bandage materials, Nekrostat™, and a detailed instruction manual. It has been specifically designed to provide holistic care for patients.
